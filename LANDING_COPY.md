## FAQ

### What do I need to enter?
Your tools, plans, spend, seat counts, team size, and primary use case.

### Do I need an account?
No. The audit is available before any email gate.

### How is the savings estimate calculated?
The core recommendations come from explicit pricing and plan-fit rules, not model hallucination.

### When do you ask for my email?
Only after the audit result is shown.

### Is this a Credex sales form?
It is a real audit tool first. Credex is surfaced when the savings opportunity is large enough to justify a conversation.
